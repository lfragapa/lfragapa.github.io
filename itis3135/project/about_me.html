About Me

Hi, I'm Madison Witham, a passionate photographer specializing in capturing life's most meaningful moments. With over five years of experience in portrait, event, and commercial photography, I've had the privilege of working with families, couples, and businesses to create images that tell their unique stories. My approach combines technical expertise with a genuine love for connecting with people, ensuring every session feels natural and comfortable. Whether it's a milestone celebration, a professional headshot, or a candid family portrait, I believe in creating timeless photographs that you'll treasure for years to come. When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me exploring new locations, experimenting with natural light, and constantly seeking inspiration in the world around me.
