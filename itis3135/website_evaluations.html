Website Evaluations

Arngren.net

This site is a pit of chaos with absolutely no organization or structure. This of course leads to a very bad user experience and makes it hard to know where to go for what you need. The only positive aspect of this site is the contrast, which is fine since nothing is hard to see due to the colors being different than their suroundings.

  • Contrast: The contrast is fine since nothing is hard to see due to the colors being to similar only from the crowdedness
  • Repetition: The only repetition on this site is the pure chaos of colors and borders, there is no color scheme or repetition of any kind
  • Alignment: There is absolutely no alignment on this site, everything is thrown together and moved arouind just to it all fits together
  • Proximity: There is no spacing between the elements, and looks so crowded

MindSparkleMag.com

This site is a very well organized and structured site. It has a consistent color scheme and has repetitive fonts and design. The site also has good spacing between all elements so it gives the site a good look and feel.This leads to a great user experience nd is visually appealing.

  • Contrast: Good contrast, everything is easy to see and no obscurity
  • Repetition: The site has a consistent color scheme and has repetitive fonts and design
  • Alignment: The site is well aligned and placeed maing it easy to navigate
  • Proximity: The site has good spacing between all elements so it gives the site a nice feel
